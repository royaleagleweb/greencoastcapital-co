We help businesses grow with confidence.

Green Coast Capital was founded on a simple belief: small-business owners deserve funding that's fast, transparent, and genuinely on their side.

Our story

Built by people who've stood in your shoes.

Green Coast Capital began in 2014 when our founders — a former community banker and a small-business owner who had been turned away one too many times — set out to build the lender they wished existed. One that looked at the health of a business, not just a credit score buried in a report.

A decade later, we've funded more than $1 billion to over 10,000 businesses across the country. Yet our mission hasn't changed: to put working capital within reach of the owners who keep our communities growing, and to do it with the patience and honesty they deserve.

  • Decisions based on real cash flow, not just a number.
  • Clear terms with no buried fees or surprises.
  • A real human advisor for every single customer.
What guides us
